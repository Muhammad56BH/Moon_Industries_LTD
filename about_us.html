Who We Are

Moon Industries, proudly Oeko-Tex Certified, has established itself as a benchmark of excellence and reliability in the textile industry. This globally recognized certification underscores our commitment to producing products that are free from harmful substances and meet the highest safety and environmental standards. Particularly in European countries, where compliance regulations are stringent, this certification has significantly boosted Moon Industries' exports. By earning the trust of global clients, we have expanded our footprint into major markets, including the United States, UK, Spain, Portugal, and other European nations.
